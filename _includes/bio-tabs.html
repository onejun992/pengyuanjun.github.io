English

PENG YUANJUN

Ph.D. (Doctor of Philosophy)

I received my Ph.D. degree from Sangmyung University, South Korea, with a specialization in Global Cultural Contents.

My research interests focus on cultural contents and cultural hybridity in East Asian contexts, with particular attention to Japanese subcultural contents and ACG (Animation, Comics, and Games) culture.

I examine the transnational circulation, localization, and hybridization processes of cultural contents in contemporary East Asia.

日本語

博士（Ph.D.）

韓国の祥明大学校（Sangmyung University）にて、グローバル文化コンテンツ学科を専攻し、博士号を取得しました。

中文

彭塬鈞（PENG YUANJUN）

博士（已获得博士学位）

我毕业于韩国祥明大学（Sangmyung University / 상명대학교）全球文化内容学科（文化内容专业），并已获得博士学位。
