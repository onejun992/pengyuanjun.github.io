## Profile

I received my Ph.D. degree from **Sangmyung University** (South Korea), specializing in **Global Cultural Contents**.

My academic research focuses on **cultural contents**, **cultural hybridity**, and **East Asian popular culture**, with particular emphasis on **Japanese subcultural contents** and **ACG (Animation, Comics, and Games)** culture.

My research interests also include **comparative cultural contents studies** across **China, Japan, and Korea**, exploring the **transnational circulation**, **localization**, and **hybridization** of cultural contents in contemporary East Asia.
